About Me

Welcome to my world of diverse passions and professional experiences! My journey has been an exciting blend of technology, agriculture, and adventure, all of which have shaped me into a versatile and driven individual.

Education

I hold a BSc in Information Technology from Northwest University (2020-2023), which has equipped me with a solid foundation in programming, software development, and IT systems. Alongside this, I have also completed a BCom in Agriculture Economics and Risk Management (2017-2020) at the same institution, with a focus on economics, risk analysis, and agricultural systems.

I expanded my skills with an online course in Programming Foundations with JavaScript, HTML, and CSS through Duke University on Coursera, which introduced me to web development fundamentals.

Professional Experience

Currently, I serve as a System Administrator at KHUSA Consulting, where I manage and optimize CRM systems, analyze business problems, and develop solutions using Python scripting and Power BI for data manipulation and reporting. Prior to this, I worked as an Administrative Consultant at KHUSA, honing my skills in client relations, data analysis, and collaboration with major medical schemes and gap cover providers.

Before stepping into the IT world, I gained valuable life skills in diverse roles—from managing teams on a construction site to assisting with planting and irrigation during farming projects. I also worked as a camp facilitator, leading outdoor activities that strengthened my people skills and taught me the art of leadership and adaptability.
